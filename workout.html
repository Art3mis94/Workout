Weekly Workout Plan

Primary training window: 17:30–18:45

Day 1 – Upper Push (17:30–18:45)

  • EZ-bar floor press – 4×8–12
  • EZ-bar overhead press – 4×8–12
  • Close-grip floor press – 3×10–12
  • Front raises – 3×12–15

Day 2 – Lower Body (17:30–18:45)

  • Barbell back squats – 4×8–12
  • EZ-bar Romanian deadlifts – 4×10
  • Reverse lunges – 3×12/leg
  • Calf raises – 3×20

Day 3 – Upper Pull (17:30–18:45)

  • Barbell bent-over rows – 4×8–12
  • EZ-bar bent-over rows – 3×10–12
  • Shrugs – 3×12–15
  • EZ-bar curls – 3×10–12

Day 4 – Cardio + Core (17:00–18:00)

  • Treadmill intervals: 30s run / 90s walk × 10
  • Plank – 3×45s
  • Leg raises – 3×12

Day 5 – Full Body Pump (17:30–18:45)

  • EZ-bar thrusters – 4×10
  • Barbell rows – 3×12
  • Skull crushers – 3×12
  • Curls – 3×12
  • RDLs – 3×10

Day 6 – Steady Cardio (16:30–17:30)

  • Incline treadmill walk – 30–40 min
  • Mobility and stretching

Day 7 – Rest

  • Light walk only (anytime)
  • Hydration and sleep focus
