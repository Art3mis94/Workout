Timing Workout Shopping Diet Plan

Routine Food Shopping List

Protein (Core)

  • Chicken breast (1.5–2 kg)
  • Lean beef mince (1–1.25 kg)
  • Eggs (24–30 pack)
  • Tinned tuna (6–10 tins)
  • Optional: salmon fillets / turkey mince

Carbohydrates

  • Protein egg noodles
  • Oats
  • Wholemeal bread
  • Kidney beans / mixed beans
  • Chopped tomatoes (tins)

Fruit & Vegetables

  • Frozen berries
  • Stir-fry vegetables (peppers, onions, mixed)
  • Leafy greens (spinach, rocket)
  • Onions
  • Garlic

Fats & Extras

  • Mayo (light or full)
  • Olive oil (small bottle)
  • Katsu curry stir-fry sauce
  • Salt & black pepper

Dairy & Drinks

  • Whole milk
  • Coffee
  • Optional: zero-sugar drinks

Optional / Flex Items

  • Dark chocolate / Kit-Kat Chunky (workout fuel)
  • Rice or microwave rice packs
  • Hot sauce / spices

Optional items are for adherence — not required, but useful.
