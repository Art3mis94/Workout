Post-Workout Dinner (7:00 PM)
Choose your protein for recovery:
Lean Beef Mince
The "Lean Power" Beef Bowl:
1. Brown 150-200g mince; drain and discard excess fat.
2. Add 1/2 diced onion and garlic. Cook until soft.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
4. Add 60g-80g fresh spinach at the end until wilted.
Serve with: 1 slice of wholemeal toast or 40g (dry weight) boiled rice.
Grilled Chicken
Simple Juicy Chicken:
1. Portion out 150g-200g of chicken breast.
2. Flatten to even thickness (use a rolling pin or heavy pan).
3. Rub with salt, pepper, and paprika.
4. Sear in a hot pan for 5-6 mins per side.
5. Let it rest for 3 mins before slicing.
Serve with: 80g steamed broccoli or peas and 1 slice of wholemeal toast.
