Overnight Blueberry & Matcha Power Bowl
The "Lean Muscle" Night-Before Prep:
1. Whisk 1-2 tsp Matcha into 250g-300g Greek Yogurt (0% or 2%).
2. Stir in 1 tbsp Chia and 1 tbsp Ground Flax.
3. Add 2-3 tbsp water/milk (crucial for seed hydration).
4. Top with 80g Frozen Blueberries. Do not stir yet.
5. Refrigerate overnight. Stir and enjoy in the morning.
No Matcha yet? Swap for 1 tsp Cinnamon or 1 tbsp Cocoa powder.
Stats: ~35g Protein | High Fiber.
Lunch Options
Tuna Sandwich on Wholemeal Bread
Pea Protein "Power Shaker" (With Powder)
Plant-Based Muscle Support:
1. Add 300ml Milk to shaker (add liquid first to prevent clumps).
2. Add 1 scoop (30g) Pea Protein Isolate.
3. Add 1 tbsp Peanut Butter and 1/2 cup Frozen Berries.
4. Shake vigorously.
Night Before: Pea protein is very absorbent. Add an extra 50ml of milk if prepping overnight to keep it liquid.
Protein: ~35-40g.
Whole Food "Dairy-Max" Smoothie (No Powder)
The Whole Food Alternative:
1. Blend 200ml Milk, 150g Greek Yogurt, and 100g Cottage Cheese.
2. Add 1 Banana or 80g berries for flavor.
3. Blend until the cottage cheese is completely smooth/creamy.
Prep Note: Great for "Night Before" as flavors meld. Shake well before drinking.
Protein: ~30-35g.

Post-Workout Dinner
Choose your protein for recovery:
Lean Beef Mince
The "Lean Power" Beef Bowl:
1. Brown 150-200g mince; drain and discard excess fat.
2. Add 1/2 diced onion and garlic. Cook until soft.
3. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
4. Add 60g-80g fresh spinach at the end until wilted.
Serve with: 1 slice of wholemeal toast or 40g (dry weight) boiled rice.
Grilled Chicken
Simple Juicy Chicken:
1. Portion out 150g-200g chicken breast.
2. Flatten for even cooking; rub with salt, pepper, and paprika.
3. Sear in hot pan for 5-6 mins per side.
4. Let it rest for 3 mins before slicing.
Serve with: 80g steamed broccoli/peas and 1 slice wholemeal toast.
