Timing Workout Shopping Diet Plan

Daily Diet Plan

Target: ~2,200–2,300 kcal · Protein 160–180g · Training window 17:30–18:45

Breakfast (07:00–09:00)

  • Primary option: Porridge bowl
    60–70g oats + 250ml whole milk + 75–100g frozen berries
  • Swap option: Poached eggs on toast
    2–3 large eggs + 1–2 slices wholemeal bread

Choose one option. Both give similar calories.

Lunch (12:00–14:00)

  • Tuna sandwich:
    1 tin tuna (120–145g drained) + 1–2 slices wholemeal bread + 1–2 tsp mayo

If especially hungry: add 1 extra slice of bread OR 1 boiled/poached egg.

Pre-Workout (16:00–17:30)

  • Black coffee (optional)
  • If hungry: 1 slice bread OR 1 egg

Optional: Kit-Kat Chunky here on hard days (counts as carbs).

Dinner / Post-Workout (18:00–20:00)

  • Chicken noodle stir-fry:
    200–250g chicken breast + 75–100g protein egg noodles (dry) + 1–2 cups stir-fry veg + Katsu sauce (measured)
  • OR beef & bean bowl:
    200–250g lean beef mince + ½ tin kidney beans + ½ tin chopped tomatoes

Heavier training day → higher noodle portion. Rest/cardio day → beans only, no noodles.

Evening (After 20:00, If Needed)

  • 1 tin tuna
  • 1–2 eggs

Protein only — no extra carbs this late.

Daily Rules (Keep It Simple)

  • Protein at every meal
  • Carbs focused around training
  • Don’t stack bread + noodles + beans
  • Measure carbs once, then eyeball
  • Water: 2.5–3.5L/day
