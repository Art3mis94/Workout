Timing Workout Shopping Diet Plan

Daily Timing & Nutrition Checklist

Training Window (Reference)

  • Primary workout time: 17:30–18:45
  • Optional black coffee: 16:00–17:30
  • Dinner / post-workout meal: 18:00–20:00
  • Cardio-only days: 16:30–17:30

Sleep & Recovery

  • Sleep 7–8 hours per night (00:00–01:00 bedtime is fine)
  • Keep wake-up time consistent
  • Avoid caffeine after 17:30
  • Light movement on rest days

Meal Timing

  • 07:00–09:00: Breakfast + coffee
  • 12:00–14:00: Lunch
  • 16:00–17:30: Optional black coffee or light protein
  • 18:00–20:00: Dinner (post-workout on training days)
  • After 20:00: Protein-only if needed

Daily Nutrition Targets

  • Calories: ~2,200–2,300/day
  • Protein: 160–180g/day
  • Carbs: Moderate, focused around training window
  • Fats: Moderate, from eggs, meat, milk
  • Water: 2.5–3.5L/day

Sample Day of Eating

  • Breakfast: Oats + milk + berries + coffee
  • Lunch: Chicken (200g) + veg OR Tuna + mayo + bread
  • Mid-afternoon: 2 eggs, milk, or tuna (optional)
  • Dinner: Beef mince or chicken + protein noodles + veg
  • Evening (if needed): Tuna or eggs

Weekly Shopping List

  • Chicken breast – 1.5–2 kg
  • Lean beef mince – 1 kg
  • Eggs – 24 pack
  • Tuna – 6–8 tins
  • Protein egg noodles
  • Stir-fry vegetables
  • Oats
  • Frozen berries
  • Whole milk
  • Optional: salmon fillets, turkey mince

Look-Your-Best Notes

  • Prioritise sleep over extra cardio
  • Keep sodium consistent day to day
  • Avoid alcohol during the 16 days
  • Maintain upright posture throughout the day
